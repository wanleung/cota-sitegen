What is HKCOTA
--------------

Hong Kong Creative Open Technology Association (HKCOTA) is a non-profit tax-exempt charity organization (Reg.# 91/12580) in Hong Kong founded in 2014 to promote
Open Standards; Free and Open Source Software; Open
Hardware; Free Culture Works, Open Content and Creative
Commons.
