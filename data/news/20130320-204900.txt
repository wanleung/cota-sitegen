<H1>自由及開源軟件在香港研討會</H1>

<P>在香港，不論是個人或團體，都有不少透過使用自由及開源軟件獲益的故事。藉著今次「國際IT匯」，香港Linux用家協會請了來自出版、資訊科技、長者服務和中學教育等的專業人士，分享他們參與自由及開源軟件的經驗。</P>

日期: 2013年4月20日(星期六)<BR/>

時間: 下午3:00-6:00<BR/>

地點: 九龍塘達之路，香港城巿大學，教學大樓四樓，LT-15演講廳<BR/>

語言：廣東話 (部份附英文演示片)<BR/>

聯絡人: 蘇孝恆博士 / info@linux.org.hk<BR/>

參加者必須在 <a href="http://registrano.com/events/hklugitfest2013/">http://registrano.com/events/hklugitfest2013/</a> 登記。<BR/>

<BR/>

<H1>Seminar on Free and Open Source Software in Hong Kong</H1>

<P>Different individuals and organisations in Hong Kong are enjoying the benefits of Free and Open Source Software. In the Hong Kong International IT Fest, professionals from area of publishing, information technology, elderly service and secondary education will share own experiences in Free and Open Source Software. </P>
Date: 20 April 2013(Sat)<BR/>

Time: 3-6pm<BR/>

Venue: LT-15, 4/F. Academic Building 1, City University of Hong Kong, Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon Tong.<BR/>

Language: Cantonese(English may be used in some slides)<BR/>

Contact: Dr. Haggen So / info@linux.org.hk<BR/>

Registration is required. Please register at <a href="http://registrano.com/events/hklugitfest2013/">http://registrano.com/events/hklugitfest2013/</a> .
