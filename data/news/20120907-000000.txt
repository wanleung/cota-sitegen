Title: 香港國際軟件自由日 2012
Content:
在九月份，我們舉辦國際軟件自由日作為本月活動，向大家的朋友宣揚軟件自由。

Date: September 15, 2012 (Saturday)
Time: 2:00-6:00pm
Venue: Classroom Y4701, 4/F City University of Hong Kong, Tat Chee Road, Kowloon Tong.
Capacity: around 40 seats.
RSVP: http://registrano.com/events/d81448?locale=en
Facebook (HKLUG): https://www.facebook.com/events/147638532042776/
Facebook (OpenSource.HK): https://www.facebook.com/events/412565702135598/
Enquiry: webmaster@opensource.hk
SFD wiki: http://wiki.softwarefreedomday.org/2012/China/Hong%20Kong/SFDHK

Agenda
- Free Software and Freedom of Speech by Dr. Haggen So. (English)
- Introduction of local and regional Open Source Communities by Mr. Sammy Fung. (English/Cantonese)
- Prediction of Legco election results using open source statistical software by Mr CH Chan.
- Unconference: please submit before start of SFD, email your topic and description to webmaster@opensource.hk first if possible.
- Panel discussion (Haggen, Sammy, guest speakers)

Thanks for Department of Computing Science, City University of Hong Kong to sponsor the venue.
